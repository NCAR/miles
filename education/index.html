Education

The MILES group has contributed to multiple initiatives for educating the broader Earth System Science community on artificial intelligence and machine learning.

NCAR AI for Earth System Science Summer School: A 1-week series of virtual lectures and a hackathon introducing machine learning, deep learning, and applications to different ESS problems.
